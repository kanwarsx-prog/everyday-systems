Case Studies

Examples of enterprise delivery and platform transformation

Diageo Salesforce Ecosystem

Problem

Diageo needed a more connected digital experience across procurement, supplier engagement and service management, replacing fragmented access points and manual support channels.

My role

I led the product strategy, architecture and delivery across Salesforce Experience Cloud, Service Cloud and integrated procurement technologies.

What was delivered

  • Procurement Hub
  • Supplier Portal
  • Service Cloud capabilities
  • Supplier Performance Management
  • Supplier segmentation
  • AI-enabled supplier intelligence
  • Integrations with SAP, Coupa, Icertis, Fairmarkit and Newgen

Outcome

A more connected enterprise platform experience for procurement teams and suppliers, improving access to information, self-service, supplier visibility and operational efficiency.
