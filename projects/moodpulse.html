Why MoodPulse?

Mental health is just as important as physical health, yet it's often harder to track and understand. Many people struggle to identify patterns in their emotional well-being, making it difficult to address underlying issues or maintain positive mental health.

MoodPulse empowers you to take control of your mental wellness through daily mood tracking, journaling, and data-driven insights. By understanding your emotional patterns, you can make informed decisions about your mental health and develop healthier coping strategies.
