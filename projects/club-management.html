The Club Management Challenge

Running a cricket club involves juggling countless tasks: coordinating player availability, scheduling matches, tracking statistics, managing payments, and keeping everyone informed. Traditional methods using spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and email chains lead to confusion, missed communications, and administrative headaches.

This platform streamlines everything into a single mobile app, making club management effortless for administrators while keeping players engaged and informed. From team selection to live match scoring, it's the complete solution for modern cricket clubs.

👥

Team Roster Management

Manage your entire squad with detailed player profiles including roles, statistics, and availability status. Filter by position (batsman, bowler, all-rounder, wicket-keeper) and track performance metrics for informed team selection.

  • ✓ Player profiles with stats
  • ✓ Role-based filtering
  • ✓ Availability tracking

Fixture Management

Schedule matches with complete details including opponent, venue, date, time, and match format (T20, ODI, Test). View upcoming fixtures and past results with scores. Automatic calendar integration and weather forecasts help with planning.

  • ✓ Upcoming & past matches
  • ✓ Weather integration
  • ✓ Calendar sync

Performance Analytics

Track comprehensive statistics for batting, bowling, and fielding. View career totals, averages, strike rates, and recent form. Visual charts show performance trends over time, helping identify strengths and areas for improvement.

  • ✓ Performance charts
