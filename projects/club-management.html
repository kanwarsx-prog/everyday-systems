The Club Management Challenge

Running a cricket club involves juggling countless tasks: coordinating player availability, scheduling matches, tracking statistics, managing payments, and keeping everyone informed. Traditional methods using spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and email chains lead to confusion, missed communications, and administrative headaches.

This platform streamlines everything into a single mobile app, making club management effortless for administrators while keeping players engaged and informed. From team selection to live match scoring, it's the complete solution for modern cricket clubs.
