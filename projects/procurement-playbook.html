The Strategy

As organizations scale globally, procurement often becomes fragmented across different regions, systems, and policies. This fragmentation leads to operational inefficiencies, loss of spend visibility, and increased risk exposure in the supply chain.

The Procurement Playbook establishes a unified, best-in-class operating model. It outlines standardized workflows, technology integration strategies (like Coupa and SAP), and AI-driven supplier intelligence methodologies to transform procurement from a cost-center into a strategic value driver.
